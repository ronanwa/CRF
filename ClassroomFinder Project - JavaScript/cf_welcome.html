Welcome to Classroom Finder!

Here you can use our Classroom Finder application to efficiently find where your class will be located on campus at Clark College!


How it works:

Enter your class item number into the textbox and click Find. This will place a marker on where your classroom is located. Below that, a summary of your class will be displayed. Click the ClassroomFinder tab to get started!
